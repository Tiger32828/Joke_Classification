Florida's beaches and blue skies are particularly charming to a traveler from the north.
The tourist was about to swim when he asked the guide, "Are you sure there are no crocodiles here?"
"No, no," the tour guide replied with a smile. "There are no crocodiles here."
No longer worried, the tourist stepped into the sea and swam. Then he asked the tour guide, "Why are you so sure there are no crocodiles?"
"The crocodile is very clever," replied the tour guide. "It is more afraid of sharks."
