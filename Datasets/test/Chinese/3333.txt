When a Taiwanese went abroad, the customs asked to open the luggage for inspection and found seven pairs of underwear.
Strangely asked why Taiwanese replied: "Sunday,Monday,Tuesday,....,Saturday"
Officials understand that it is one a day.

Then came a Frenchman, and the officer asked to open the luggage and check that there were five pairs of underwear.
Strangely asked why the French replied: "Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday"
How is Saturday and Sunday?
NOWEAR.

Officials understand that the French are romantic and don't wear it on Saturdays and Sundays.
Then an Indian came, and the inspection found that there were twelve pairs of underwear. The official was puzzled and asked how?
The Indian replied slowly: "January, February, March, April..."
