A man with a broken arm was admitted to the hospital emergency room.
The doctor asked, "How did your hand get hurt?"
Patient: "It's all because there are rocks in the shoes!"
The doctor asked inexplicably, "There is a stone in the shoe, why is the hand broken?"
The patient replied helplessly: "Walking on the road, a stone ran into the shoe,
Because I felt uncomfortable, there happened to be a telephone pole next to me, so I put my hand on the pole,
His feet kept shaking, hoping to shake the stone out. Just then a passerby passed by.
He thought I was electrocuted, so he hit me hard with a wooden stick..."
