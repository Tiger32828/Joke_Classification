A patient came to see the doctor with two injured ears covered.
The doctor first examined his left ear and asked him how he still injured it.
He said, "I was busy ironing my clothes with an electric iron when the phone rang. In a hurry, I picked up the iron and used it as a phone..."
The doctor said sympathetically, "How unfortunate! Then how did your right ear get burned?"
The patient replied angrily, "While I was in so much pain over my left ear injury, that nasty idiot called again..."
