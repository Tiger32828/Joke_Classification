I was watching TV at home that day, and it was talking about how children deal with danger.
So, I wanted to test my two-year-old son.
I pretended to faint, "Mom, mom, what's wrong with you?" my son shouted.
And he opened my eyes with his hands: "Mom, are you really dead?"
After waiting for a while, no movement was heard.
Through squinted eyes, I saw my son counting the money in my wallet...
