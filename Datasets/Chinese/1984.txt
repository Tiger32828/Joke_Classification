There was a company looking for a female secretary, and four women came to apply...
The general manager asked everyone a question: "Name the purpose of the upper and lower 'mouths'."
The first one said: "The upper mouth is for eating, and the bottom is for drinking 'milk'."
The second said, "The top is horizontal, and the bottom is straight."
The third said, "The upper mouth does not bleed, and the lower mouth bleeds once a month."
The fourth said: "The top port is for my own use, and the bottom port is for the general manager."

As a result, the general manager admitted a fourth woman...
