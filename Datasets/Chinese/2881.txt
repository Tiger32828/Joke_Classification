A thief was put on a shackle by the government and displayed to the public, and pedestrians gathered around to watch.
An acquaintance was surprised when he saw it, and he stepped forward and asked, "Why did you commit such a big crime?"
He sighed: "Oh, when people carry their backs, everything is unlucky. Yesterday I was walking on the street, and I happened to see a straw rope on the ground. I thought that it might be useful in the future, so I picked it up. that is......"
The acquaintance asked inexplicably: "Is there such a thing? Can picking up a straw rope be sentenced to such a serious crime?"
The prisoner hesitated: "Who knows: ... a calf is tied to the straw rope!"
