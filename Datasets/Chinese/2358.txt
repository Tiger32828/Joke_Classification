One day on the bus, there are many people on the bus, and I finally fell asleep when I had a seat.
In a daze, I was woken up by the brakes. I felt that someone was standing beside me with a child in my arms, and immediately stood up from the seat.
He said loudly, "You hold the child and give you your seat. Hurry up and sit down!"
All the people in the car turned their attention, and saw a little girl beside me with a blushing face, holding a stuffed toy...
