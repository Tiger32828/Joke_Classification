The young man asked the Zen master, "What should I do if my heart is filled with worries and troubles?"
The Zen master said thoughtfully, "You draw a curved line at random. Use a magnifying glass to enlarge it. Isn't its surroundings very clear and open?"

The young man drew a Peano curve.
(The Peano curve can traverse all the points in the unit square and is a curve that fills space.)
