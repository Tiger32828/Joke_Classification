Fat daughter: "Mom! Why did you make me so fat that no one chased me. I see, I'll just go to sea!"
Mother: "Are you going to sea? Then, I think all those men will go ashore!"
Fat daughter: "Mom! Why do you say that? Then...I don't want to live! I'm going to kill myself! I'm going to burn myself to ashes, so that you will never recognize me!"
Mother: "Haha! How could I not recognize you? Just look for the biggest pile of ashes—"
