One day, the teacher saw Xiaoxin eating chewing gum while he was in class.
The teacher called him up and scolded him for not chewing gum in class.
But Xiaoxin said no, and the teacher checked his mouth and found that it was really empty.
After a while, Xiaoxin's mouth was chewing again, so the teacher called him out again, but he still couldn't find the chewing gum.
On the third time, the teacher couldn't help it and asked him, "I won't punish you, but you want to tell me, what the hell are you chewing?"
Xiaoxin said: "I have a cold recently, and the phlegm is sticky..."
