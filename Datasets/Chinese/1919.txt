A buddy went to interview a salesperson of electronic products. After all aspects of testing, an interviewer took out a repeater and asked the buddy, "Who is the best to sell this product to?"
That buddy didn't even think about saying, "It's best to sell to a national diplomat."
The interviewer was stunned for a long time before asking "Why?"
The buddy replied, "No matter what happens to our country and foreign countries, how much we suffer, how much insults, the diplomat's answer is: 'Strongly protest, seriously condemn, and pay close attention to the development of this matter.' So the diplomat only needs to If you buy a repeater, you can solve the world's major issues."

No need to guess, that buddy was admitted.
