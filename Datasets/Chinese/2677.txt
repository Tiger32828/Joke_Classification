Mr. Tom couldn't sleep for eight days and eight nights, and all kinds of medicines were ineffective. In desperation, the family had to call a famous hypnotist.
Staring closely at Mr. Tom and saying, "You are asleep, Mr. Tom! The darkness is over you, the soft music is putting you to sleep, you are asleep! You are asleep!"
The worried family looked at the patient, who no doubt fell asleep, and his son gratefully said to the hypnotist, "You are a miracle worker!"
Then he was paid a handsome sum, and the hypnotist walked away triumphantly.
Just as the door closed, Mr. Tom opened one eye. "Hello!" he asked. "Is the madman gone?"
