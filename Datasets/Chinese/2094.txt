Principal's instruction:
The school entered a young teacher, and the principal said to him earnestly:
You have to be nice to a student with a score of 100, and he will become a scientist in the future;
You have to be nice to a student with a score of 80, he may be a colleague with you;
You have to be nice to a student who fails the exam, and he will donate money to the school in the future;
You have to be nice to the student who cheats in the exam, he will enter politics in the future;
You have to be nice to the classmate who dropped out in the middle, he will become Bill Gates or Jobs.
