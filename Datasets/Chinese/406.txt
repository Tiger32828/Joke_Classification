The life of the modern elderly is like this:

1. The days are good, live like a New Year every day, called: live like a year
2. You don't need to take banknotes when you go to the street, just scan it with your mobile phone, it's called: penniless.
3. Interest dividends are entered into the bank passbook on time every month, which is called: waiting for money.
4. They all live in cement-structured buildings, called: a house without tiles.
5. Traveling around when you are free from retirement is called: living without a fixed place, wandering around.
