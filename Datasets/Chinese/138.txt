One day, two female classmates went out to play together. Since the trip had to be overnight, they decided to sleep in a double room together.
One of the girls sang very nicely, so the other girl never dared to sing in front of her.
That night, she really wanted to sing, but she was afraid that her roommate would hear it, so she thought, "Then I sing secretly in the shower, and no one will know!"
She secretly sang in a low voice while the shower head was running loudly.
After taking a shower, her roommate nervously asked her, "Did you hear anything just now?" She thought to herself, "Has it been discovered!?" So she denied it...
The roommate's expression was almost crying, and he said, "This room doesn't seem to be clean. When you were taking a shower just now, I heard a ghost crying..."
