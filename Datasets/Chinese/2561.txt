A certain boy has been in love with a girl for a long time, but he has not dared to confess it, because the girl has an elegant temperament, so he does not dare to be casual.
Until one day, the boy finally summoned up the courage to confess to her: "Miss, I have been paying attention to you for a long time. Your noble temperament deeply attracted me, can I be your friend?"
The girl turned her head gracefully and said softly, "Your grandmother has been waiting for you for a long time!"
