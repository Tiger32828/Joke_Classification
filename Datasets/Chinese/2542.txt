A court opened to hear a rape case involving the daughter of a high-ranking government official.
Prosecutors told the victim if she could identify the offender who raped her.
Pointing to a young man in the dock, she said, "It's him, that's right! It's him—"
So the prosecutor asked her again, when did she get raped? Ask her if she can remember clearly?
The victim pondered for a moment before replying, "It was January, February, March, April, May, June of last year... Who told him not to marry me—"
