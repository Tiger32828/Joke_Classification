One day Xiao Ming's father bought him a motorcycle. Xiao Ming's father went to Guan Laoye Temple specially to ask Guan Laoye to bless him not to have an accident.
Soon, Xiao Ming had a car accident, and Xiao Ming's father went to Guan Laoye Temple and cursed, "Why don't you bless my son?"
In the end, Master Guan asked Xiao Ming's father in a dream and said, "It's not that I don't bless your son, it's your son who rides 180, and my red rabbit horse only runs 50..."
