The sufferer asks:
"Master, if you have a strong heart, you will be able to not be promiscuous in the prosperous world.
Not depressed in the madness of adversity, calm and calm in the face of difficulties and pressures, and calm in the face of contempt and humiliation.
Can't imagine what life will be like when you reach such a state? "
The Zen master pointed to the ancient tree in the forest.
The suffering person suddenly said, "Ah, is it strong, stable, and tolerant like a big tree?"
