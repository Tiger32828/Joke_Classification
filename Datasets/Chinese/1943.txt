There is a doctor with poor medical skills, and few people seek medical treatment from him.
One day, someone suddenly came to buy medicine. He opened the box to get the medicine, and found that there were moths inside. The buyer saw it and asked, "What is this?"
The doctor replied, "The silkworm can be used as medicine."
The medicine buyer asked again, "How could the silkworm be alive?"
The doctor said, "Because it took my medicine..."
