After class at noon, I went to the cafeteria with a dinosaur girl in the class together with a flower umbrella.
Maybe it was the drizzle, she suddenly stopped and looked at me affectionately, "Will you... be my prince?"
Looking at her miserable face, I really can't bear to break her heart, but for future happiness, I must refuse.
So, I put on a silly and innocent expression and asked her, "Then... I'll have to call you your mother in the future?"
