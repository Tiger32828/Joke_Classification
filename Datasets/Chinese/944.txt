One day, a bank branch was broken into by gangsters
Twenty million dollars stolen
The bank manager did not call the police for the first time
Instead, gather the crew
The manager thought about it for a while, and remembered that he had embezzled 30 million public funds before.
So I said to everyone present: "If someone asks you after you go out later, you can say that you were robbed of 100 million yuan, and the extra 50 million yuan will be shared equally with everyone!"
After that, major media reported the sensational "100 million yuan robbery"
This report was also transferred to the hands of the robbers and his party.
The leader of the robber spat and said angrily, "We are born and die, and we have worked hard to get 20 million yuan; when they sit in the office, they get 100 million yuan just by talking!"
