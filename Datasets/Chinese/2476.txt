Mr. Pat invited a doctor into the house to see his wife, and he invited the doctor into the house and waited outside the door.
Soon, the doctor stuck his head out and asked, "Mr. Pat, do you have a screwdriver?"
After a while, the doctor asked for forceps again. Later, a hammer. Mr. Pat finally couldn't bear it any longer: "Doctor, what's wrong with my wife?"
The doctor said, "I don't know, my medicine chest hasn't been opened yet!"
