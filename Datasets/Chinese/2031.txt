The fire is very violent, and the consequences will be unimaginable if there is no strong attack.

Upon seeing this, the fire chief immediately ordered his staff: "Go and find water!"
After the staff received the order, they flew away immediately.
After a while, the fire chief saw that the fire was getting bigger and bigger, and he was in a hurry when he suddenly heard the voices of staff members in the distance: "Water is coming! Water is coming!"
At this time, the big stone in the fire chief's heart was finally put down, and he turned around and was preparing to deploy a fire water line to attack the fire point, only to see the staff members panting and holding a box of "mineral water": "Report Captain, Here comes the water!"
