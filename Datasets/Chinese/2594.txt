On their wedding night, A-De and A-Ling came to the honeymoon suite they booked. A-Ling found a small box beside the bed. She asked in confusion, "What is this for?"
"If you drop a fifty-dollar coin," Ed replied, reaching into his pocket, "the bed would shake."
"Don't spend the fifty dollars in vain!" A Ling stopped him and said, "I will move too."
