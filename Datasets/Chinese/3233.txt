One day, Li Xiucai, who has always been fond of dancing and writing, saw a farmer holding a large basket and a small one.
He said, "The big basket is also the basket, and the small basket is also the basket.
After hearing this, the farmer did not know how to answer, but a funeral procession happened to pass by.
The farmer went on to say, "Scholars are also talents, and coffins are also materials. Scholars sleep in coffins, and two talents are one material..."
