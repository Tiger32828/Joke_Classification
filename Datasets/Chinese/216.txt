Xiao Ming went to the night market for supper.
When I saw "Pasta with Flower Branches" written on the sign, I ordered a bowl to eat it.
After eating half of it, Xiao Ming found that there were no flower branches at all, and he felt cheated!
I asked the lady boss, "My lady, why is there no sticks in my pasta?"
The proprietress: "Huazhi is my name!"
