On the Chung Yuan Festival, I took my five-year-old son to a Buddhist temple to worship~
When the car turned into the only way to the Buddhist temple, a nun was seen walking towards the Buddhist temple. I thought that there was still a long way to go from the Buddhist temple, so I might as well give her a ride~
So he said to the son next to him, "Later Mommy opens the car window, and you invite that aunt to come and sit and call her "Auntie Bhikshuni"!
After a while, the car drove to the nun's side, rolled down the window, and saw his son shouting happily: "Aunt Pikachu, my mother said please..."
