"When I die..." the rich man told his young wife, "I want you to remarry."
"Don't say that..." she said.
"Give him all my clothes and let him look good when he takes you out," he said.
"Don't keep talking about it!" she cried, "how can that be?"
"Why not?" he asked her.
"You wear a medium..." she explained to him. "He wears a plus size."
