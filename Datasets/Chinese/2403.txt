A novice goes to collect loan sharks.
He took out the IOU and said with a wicked smile, "It's written in black and white that you owe my boss 1 million! Do you want to default on your debt?!"
People said that they really didn't have that much money, and he threatened: "Hum! Don't blame me for not reminding you! If you can't pay tomorrow, your house will look like it."
He took out his lighter and burned the loan...
