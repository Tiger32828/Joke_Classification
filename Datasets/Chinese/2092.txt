After school, I had to wait for the bus. It was crowded like a can of sardines. I managed to squeeze in and grab a seat next to the driver.
In the chaos, the driver suddenly braked suddenly at a corner. In a hurry, I had to hold on to the change box. I didn't expect that the change box that had been in disrepair for a long time would be pulled up by me like this...
All the passengers in the car looked at me and laughed; and the driver also spoke at this time: "Miss, we are doing a small business, please be merciful?"
