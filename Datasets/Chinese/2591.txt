A man with a broken leg decided to tell his wife this fact on his wedding night.
When the two were alone in the room, the man said, "There will be something surprising you later, please don't be afraid."
After he finished speaking, he turned off the light, and in the dark he led his wife's hand to the thigh where he had removed his prosthesis.
At this point, the wife said, "Ah! I was really surprised, but I can probably stand it..."
