It is said that back then, our young husband and wife were forced to marry each other by the adults' phone calls from one day to the next.
One day, when I was surfing the Internet for some reason, I felt very annoyed, so I turned around and said to my husband who was lying on the bed reading, "Oh, forget it, don't you just get a certificate? We'll get it tomorrow!"
Then my husband suddenly covered his face with the book in his hand and said shyly: "You hate it, the marriage proposal in people's dreams is not like this..."
