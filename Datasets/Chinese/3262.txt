A man leaves home to go to work on a Friday afternoon, which is payday,
So instead of going home, he spent the weekend out partying with friends and spending his entire salary.
When he finally got home on Sunday night, his fiery wife was waiting for him.
He scolded him for what he had done in a barrage of fire for nearly an hour.
Finally, the wife stopped chattering and asked him, "How would you feel if you couldn't see me for three days in a row?"
"I feel pretty good," he replied.
Monday passed and he didn't see his wife.
Tuesday and Wednesday passed, and he still hadn't seen his wife.
By Thursday, the swelling had subsided a little, and he could finally barely see his wife from the corner of his left eye...
