11 people were shipwrecked, and they were rescued by helicopter with ropes until the ship sank. …
Eleven men and one woman were all hanging from the ropes of a helicopter.
The helicopter was about to crash because the rope couldn't support everyone's weight, so they discussed that one person must let go or everyone would be screwed.
These men can't decide who to let go....
Until the woman said touchingly: "Of course I will let go voluntarily, because we women are used to giving up everything for the sake of children and husbands, giving everything to men without asking for anything in return, really... "

When she finished speaking, all the men began to applaud, so in the end only the woman survived...
