It is said that computer class is a compulsory subject for today's students, but for Xiao Der, it is a memory that he will never forget for the rest of his life.

One day, the teacher on the stage gushed about how to use the computer:
"Come~ everyone, please move the mouse to the lower right corner of the screen now."
Everyone moves the mouse, but...
I saw Daddy alone "pick up" the mouse and "put" it in the lower right corner of the screen.
