A young hunter came to ask an old hunter how to fight a bear.
The old hunter said: "Usually find a cave first, and then throw a stone into the cave. If you hear a low roar, there must be a bear in it. At this time, you must jump to the entrance of the cave and shoot inside. You must be able to Hit the bear."

A few days later, the old hunter saw the young hunter in the hospital with bandages all over his body.
"That day..." the young hunter said, "I followed your teaching and found a large cave, and then threw a stone into it, and I really heard a low and huge roar from inside. I thought, this must be a big guy, so I jumped to the hole..."
"Shoot now!" said the old hunter.
"But before I could shoot, a train drove out of the cave..."
