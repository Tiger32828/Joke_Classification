A beautiful woman in a miniskirt meets a hooligan on the bus. The hooligan said, "Miss, let me see your thighs! I'll give you 50 yuan."
The woman said, "Well, give me 100 yuan first, and when the bus arrives, I'll show you the place where I gave birth."
The hooligan was overjoyed.
When the bus arrived at the stop, she pointed to the roadside hospital and said, "Look, this is where I gave birth!"
