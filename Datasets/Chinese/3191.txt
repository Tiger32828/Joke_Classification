The boss of a large firm bought some motto boards with the words "Don't Hesitate!"
Hang it prominently around the office, hoping to encourage employees to work faster and more energetically.
A few days later, a friend asked him if it had worked.
"Alas, not ideal," the boss said. "The cashier absconded with $30,000, the sales director eloped with my personal secretary, and three staff members asked for a raise."
