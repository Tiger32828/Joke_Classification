When the papal ambassador was in France, the future Pope John XXIII was also invited to France to attend the feast.
At the banquet, a scantily clad lady happened to be sitting next to John XXIII, and he could only pretend not to notice the lady's dress.
When the sweets came, he picked a red apple and handed it to the lady, but she politely declined.
"Have a taste, ma'am," he advised. "It was after eating the forbidden fruit that Eve realized she was naked."
