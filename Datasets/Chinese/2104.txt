Knowing that his wife had betrayed him, the husband asked his wife how it happened.
The wife calmly recounted what happened: "One day, a young man in rags and hungry broke into our house. I saw how pitiful he looked, so I treated him well. Let him take a bath, eat, and give your wine to me. After he drank it, he even put your clothes and shoes on for him. While grateful, he asked me if there was anything else my husband didn't need? I suddenly remembered that you haven't used me for a long time, so I... .."
