I worked overtime with a colleague (male) to catch up on a project at night, and it was done after 12:00 in the morning. When I went downstairs, the building was empty, chatting while waiting for the elevator.
After a while, the elevator came down, and when the door opened, there was actually someone inside! It is a girl (it is estimated that she is working overtime to leave so late).
To be honest, the two of us were taken aback. We didn't expect to meet someone in the elevator so late, and we were stunned for a moment.
The most bizarre thing was my colleague. I saw him looking at the elevator and saying, "Damn, it's full of people, we can't squeeze any more, let's wait for the next ride."
I nodded expressionlessly.
The moment the elevator door closed, the girl's face was pale...
