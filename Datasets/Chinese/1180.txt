At night, the girl sat sadly under the plane tree, missing two men.
One is her college classmate, handsome and well-recognized as the school grass. She pursued her for three years, but she did not agree.
The other is his cousin, who is stable and temperamental. He has been a childhood sweetheart since childhood, and has taken good care of her.

Every time she thinks of this, the girl can't help but sigh and mutter to herself, "If I knew earlier, I wouldn't have introduced them to each other..."
