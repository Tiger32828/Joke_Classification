Grandma is in her seventies, so she has dentures.
One day, I was reading in the study room on the first floor. When grandma came down from the second floor,
She sneezed so loudly! As a result, I saw an arc-shaped thing fly out!
Looking closely, it turned out that it was grandma's dentures, which fell off because of too much force!
It made me roll on the ground with laughter on the spot, and my grandmother had to smile awkwardly. …
