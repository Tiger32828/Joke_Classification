A quack doctor misdiagnosed someone's son and died. In order to compensate, he gave his son to the other party as an adopted son.
Then, he diagnosed another person's maid to death. In order to compensate, he gave his maid to the other party.
One night, someone knocked on the door of his house and said, "Doctor, it's not good. My wife has sudden abdominal pain. Please see a doctor immediately."
The doctor called his wife out and reluctantly said, "Wife, you have to be mentally prepared this time..."
