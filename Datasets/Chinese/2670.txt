Reverend Weipier preached to the Indians at Minifence, and on the first day he was going to a nearby village to carry out his duties, so he left in a hurry, leaving many things behind the chief of that tribe. in the house.
"Will these things be safe after I leave?" he asked the chief.
"Oh! Please don't worry about them," the chief replied. "There is no white man within a hundred miles."
