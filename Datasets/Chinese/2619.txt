The husband is on a business trip abroad, and the wife is clamoring to go together.
Mr.: "My dear, I'm only on a business trip for three days. Why do you have to come with me?"
Wife: "I want to go abroad to buy clothes."
Mr.: "Buy clothes? Please, can you go to Shin Kong Mitsukoshi or SOGO? There are a lot of clothes there too!"
Wife: "Thank you husband!"
