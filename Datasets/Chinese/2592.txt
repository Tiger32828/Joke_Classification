A newlywed couple went to a certain European castle for their honeymoon, and the wife came up with an interesting idea: as long as the castle bell rings every time, the couple will have sex once.
On the first night, they did have sex every time they struck the bell.
The next day, the husband thought, this is not the way, sooner or later he will be exhausted.
So, he went to the person in charge of ringing the bell in the castle: "What: ... Mr., I want to ask you a favor.
Here's 5,000 yuan, you can take it, can you change the bell to ring every two hours tonight?
Because my wife said this and that..., you know, exhausted me..."
The bell ringer said: "I'm really embarrassed, my wife has stipulated that I can only rest when the bell is ringing..."
