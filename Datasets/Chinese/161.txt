A certain A went to camp with a certain B. When he went to bed that night, when A was half asleep, B was shaken up.
Ask B: "What do you think when you look up?"
B said: "The sky is full of stars, it makes me feel that this world is so vast! The sky is so vast, I can't help but sigh how small people are!!"
After B finished watching A staring at the sky for so long without saying a word, he asked A, "What do you think?"


A solemnly said to B, "I think our tent has been stolen..."
