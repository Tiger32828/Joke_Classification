At a wedding chapel in Las Vegas, the groom asked the pastor how much he would pay after the wedding.
The pastor replied, "We do not charge for this kind of service!"
Then, the humorous pastor joked: "However, many grooms donate according to the beauty of the bride. The more beautiful the bride, the more the groom will donate."
So the groom gave the pastor a dollar and a dollar.
As a result, after looking at the bride for a while, the priest put his hand into his pocket and said, "I'm afraid I'll find you fifty cents..."
