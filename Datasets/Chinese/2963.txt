A teacher told the story of "Kong Rong Letting Pears" in class, and asked students to write down the motives of Kong Rong letting pears.
In the answer sheet handed in, the answers can be divided into four main categories:
1. The pear is rotten;
2. Kong Rong had a toothache at that time;
3. This is so that the person who takes the pear can help him with his homework;
4. To become famous.
The teacher looked embarrassed.
