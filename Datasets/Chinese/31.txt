Near the New Year's Day, various gift companies distributed many exquisite leaflets to each company to attract business, and the incident was caused by a super-exquisite leaflet.
The stingy Xiao Wang took a beautiful flyer to a female colleague and asked her to fax it to his brother who works in the north.
And write a small line on the flyer: "Brother, this thing looks very good, I will fax the product to you, please go buy it, take it home and enjoy it with your parents!"
The next day, Xiao Wang received a message from his elder brother: "Brother, this thing looks really good, but I can't go back to my hometown in the south this holiday. So I faxed two thousand-yuan bills to you, and I'll ask you to cut it. Buy it yourself!"
